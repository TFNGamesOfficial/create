DevNite Creative - V1.10 Update

Creative is here! Learn what you can do and the future of creative.


Creative mode was released with the v1.10 update! Here is an overview of the features and the future


Features

Creative has many features but in this update log we will show you the basics / core features.


Copy and Delete objects

You can copy objects by pressing the shooting key on your mouse or controller. To delete objects press the delete key on your keyboard or the emote key on your controller.


Place and Move objects

You can place objects by opening your inventory with the tab key or the inventory key on controller. In the inventory you'll see Galleries, Prefabs and Devices. Select your objects and hold the interaction key. When placing the objects you can adjust the grid snap and more. To place it press the shooting key on mouse or controller.
To move an object press the aim key on mouse or controller and move your character until you have the perfect position.
